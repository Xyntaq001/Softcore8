Our Research Community

Curiosity Meets Collaboration

Research at Kenyatta University lies at the heart of our commitment to discovery, innovation, and societal progress. We foster a research culture that encourages curiosity and creative thinking across all disciplines.

Our research community is supported by cutting-edge facilities, specialized laboratories, and interdisciplinary research centers that promote collaboration beyond traditional academic boundaries — whether exploring frontiers of AI, developing sustainable energy solutions, or studying the human condition.

In the Lab & Field

Research in Action

Students in Research

Learn by Discovering

We place a strong emphasis on student involvement in research. Undergraduate and graduate students alike are encouraged to take part in meaningful research experiences, where they can work side-by-side with faculty mentors and contribute to major projects.

Students present their findings at conferences and publish in peer-reviewed journals. These experiences enhance learning and prepare students for careers in academia, industry, and beyond.
