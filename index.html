Our Story

A Community Built on Excellence

At Kenyatta University, we are more than an institution of higher learning — we are a community dedicated to academic excellence, innovation, and global impact. Since our founding in 1955, we have been committed to shaping future leaders.

Our academic programs span the arts and humanities, sciences, engineering, business, health, and beyond, providing students with the tools to think critically, communicate effectively, and solve real-world problems.
