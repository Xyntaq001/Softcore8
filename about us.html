About Kenyatta University

Our Mission

More Than an Institution

At Kenyatta University, we are more than an institution of higher learning — we are a community dedicated to academic excellence, innovation, and global impact. Since our founding in 1955, we have been committed to shaping future leaders, advancing research that matters, and building a more inclusive and informed society.

Located in the heart of Nairobi, Kenya, Kenyatta University offers a vibrant and diverse campus experience, with a student body representing cultures, perspectives, and talents from across the country and around the world.

Our academic programs span the arts and humanities, sciences, engineering, business, health, and beyond, providing students with the tools to think critically, communicate effectively, and solve real-world problems.

Our faculty are internationally recognized experts, scholars, and mentors who bring their passion for teaching and research into every classroom, lab, and community partnership.

At Kenyatta University, we don't just educate students — we empower them to lead, discover, and inspire. Welcome to a place where knowledge meets purpose and education becomes transformation.
